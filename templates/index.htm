Easy Solutions

We've designed CaptionCatch to make getting YouTube transcripts simple. One click is all it takes to turn videos into text easily.

Simply paste your YouTube video URL into CaptionCatch, and click the "Extract Captions" button. Our tool instantly processes the video and provides you with a complete transcript that you can copy, download, or share with a single click.

CaptionCatch works with any YouTube video that has captions or subtitles available. This includes both auto-generated captions and manually added subtitles. If a video doesn't have captions, our tool cannot extract what isn't there.

CaptionCatch allows you to download transcripts in multiple formats including TXT, SRT, and PDF. You can also copy the text directly to your clipboard for immediate use in your projects or documents.
